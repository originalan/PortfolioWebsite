LEGO Project
Designing and creating a full LEGO Guillotine set from scratch, instructions included.
↓
Aren't LEGOs easy?
Yes, but don't be fooled. They are surprisingly hard to design, especially from scratch. Our team was tasked with creating a full LEGO set, a daunting task. With little hand-holding, we came up with the idea of a Guillotine, inspired by the French Revolution.

We had to prototype base LEGO bricks to test dimensions that would fit well with each other. In the end, on top of the physical model, we made an instruction booklet, technical drawings, and packaging to sell the idea that it was a real LEGO set. Pieces were modeled after real LEGOs but many were also custom-made for our set.
